About Us

We are a group of purpose-driven individuals with unbeatable IT expertise and an open-minded attitude to provide you with cutting-edge solutions.

Freshcode is a UK registered company with the Software Development Hub in Eastern Europe, in one of the most important technical/technology areas in Europe  (aviation, automotive, IT), Zaporizhzhya, Ukraine.
The perfect combination of important industry leaders in aviation and automotive, historical German influence, with strong educational environment (Polytechnic University, Technical Academy, Medicine University) makes the city an important alternative for the companies looking to expand their software development capabilities.
